Dane Reinhart

Background and Experience

Dane Reinhart is a real estate financial and investment professional with nearly a decade of experience working for institutional real estate owner/operator groups such as Hayden Properties, Presidium and Balboa Real Estate Partners, where he led projects in the financial analysis, acquisition, disposition, development and asset management of residential and commercial real estate assets.

His proven track record demonstrates his ability to identify and capitalize on lucrative real estate opportunities. Dane has a comprehensive understanding of the entire real estate investment life cycle, from deal sourcing and due diligence to project execution and asset management, where his strong analytical skills and financial acumen enable him to make sound investment decisions that maximize returns for stakeholders.

Stakeholder Priority

Dane understands the financial feasibility and investment potential of development projects. He is an expert in Proforma Evaluation of real estate opportunities with Return on Investment and Internal Rate of Return thresholds that meet our company standards and requirements. Likewise, he performs Cash-Flow Analysis to track and monitor the progress of each residential subdivision land entitlement, projected double-escrow closing dates and profit distributions to stakeholders. This expertise allows our company to advise stakeholders on maximizing the return on their investments, ensuring projects are not only feasible, but also strategically positioned for success.

Professional Credentials

Additionally, Dane's experience extends beyond traditional real estate project financial analysis. He possesses a deep understanding of the land entitlement process, having secured necessary approvals for various development projects.

This expertise allows him to evaluate the feasibility of raw land acquisitions and navigate the complexities of Land Use and Zoning Regulations, approval projections to streamline project timelines, mitigate potential risks associated with development delays and keep projects on time.
